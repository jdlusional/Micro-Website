On the Nature of Things

On the Nature of Things
after Lucretius, De Rerum Natura

An essay publication on policy, philanthropy, and the business of thinking for a living.

Read on Substack

The title borrows from Lucretius, who set out to explain the whole universe in verse and mostly pulled it off.

The ambition here is smaller but the spirit is the same: to think clearly, in public, about how institutions and ideas actually work. The publication is where my research, policy analysis, and consulting meet a wider audience, translated out of jargon and into plain argument.

The writing tends toward the analytical and the curious: essays that take an idea seriously, follow it where it leads, and treat the reader as a fellow thinker. It is, in the most literal sense, the business of thinking for a living, made legible.
