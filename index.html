Our practice

Core subjects of expertise

MICRO names the five focuses that guide every engagement. Each stands on its own or combines into a solution built for your institution.

MManagement+
Strategic management and planning, executive leadership development, organizational strategy formulation, and interim director services. We ensure your institution is guided by effective leadership and management practices that align with your mission and vision.
IIntegration+
Programmatic and organizational integration, software implementation, and organization-wide training. We streamline operations, improve data management, and prepare your staff to leverage new systems and processes.
CCulture+
Organizational culture development, team building, and employee engagement. We help define and cultivate a culture that aligns with your values and strengthens collaboration, communication, and cohesion across your organization.
RResearch+
Organizational research services including employee pay analysis, market analyses, and policy impact studies. We equip you with the data necessary to make informed, evidence-based decisions that move your organization forward.
OOperations+
Advanced operational management solutions that increase productivity, optimize resource allocation, and enhance overall institutional effectiveness, allowing you to meet your specific objectives with the resources you have.
