Founder & CEO

Jonathan Lin Davis is the Founder and CEO of MICRO Group, LLC. After nearly a decade of professional consultative work, he founded MICRO in 2023 as a culmination of that work and to professionalize his services, with the mission to provide strategic, research-driven consultative solutions across the government, nonprofit, and educational spaces.

He began his career in the government and educational sectors, serving as Executive Director of the Generation Hope Program under the leadership of Tarrant County Commissioner Roy C. Brooks. Early board and commission service gave him an exceptional vantage on how institutions work and function. Throughout his professional journey he has led, managed, and founded various institutions, including MEASURE Austin, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit research and public education organization he co-founded in 2017.

His educational background includes an Associate's in Business Management from Tarrant County College, a Bachelor's in Political Science from Texas Christian University, a Master of Public Administration from the University of Texas at Arlington, and a Master of Public Affairs from the LBJ School of Public Affairs at the University of Texas at Austin. He is currently completing an Ed.D. at Vanderbilt University, and holds additional certificates in budgeting, mediation, philanthropy, and grants management.

His research has been supported through fellowships with the Population Research Center and the RGK Center for Philanthropy and Community Service at the University of Texas at Austin, and as a Nonprofit Austin Fellow in Policy and Practice.
