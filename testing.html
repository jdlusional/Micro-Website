Nonprofit Austin (NPA) job board prototype, four connected views. The frames are live and same-origin, so navigating within one frame works as it would on the real site.

Job Board The main public listing where visitors browse and filter open nonprofit roles.
Job Detail A single posting expanded, showing the full description and how to apply.
Post a Job The employer form for submitting a new role to the board.
Admin Review The behind-the-scenes view for approving, editing, or removing submitted postings.
